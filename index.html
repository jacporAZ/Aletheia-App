Why we exist

Dating apps forgot to put the people in.

We keep meeting avatars. Filtered photos, rehearsed prompts, conversations that collapse the moment they leave the app. Aletheia is built on the boring, unsexy idea that dating works better when you know the other person exists — and that they want to meet you.

How it works

Three rules. That's it.

Verify who you are.

Meet ten people.

Call, then chat.
