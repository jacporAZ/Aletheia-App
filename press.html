About Aletheia

Aletheia (ἀλήθεια — Greek for "truth") is a verification-first dating app founded in Brooklyn in 2026. Every user is ID-verified, every match requires a video call before messaging, and matches are capped at ten per day. Aletheia is currently in TestFlight.
